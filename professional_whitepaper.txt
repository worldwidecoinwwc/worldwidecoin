3. MONETARY POLICY
===============================================================================

The WWC monetary policy is designed around a continuous emission decay model rather than 
discrete halving events.

Traditional halving models reduce block rewards abruptly, which can create instability in 
miner revenue and network hash power.

WorldWideCoin instead reduces rewards gradually using exponential decay. Block reward follows 
the function:

R(t) = R₀ × e^(-kt)

Where:

R(t) = block reward at time t
R₀ = initial block reward
k = annual decay constant
t = time in years

For WWC:

R₀ = 1.0
k = 0.025 (2.5%)

This produces smooth reward reduction and avoids sudden changes in miner incentives.


4. SUPPLY MODEL
===============================================================================

Total supply can be derived from the integral of the block reward function over time.

S(t) = ∫ R(t) × B dt

Where:

B = blocks per year

Solving the equation gives:

S(t) = (R₀ × B / k) × (1 - e^(-kt))

As time approaches infinity:

S_max = R₀ × B / k

Substituting parameters:

S_max ≈ 21,024,000 WWC

Thus, the system approaches a long-term supply close to 21 million coins.
